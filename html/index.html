About Unlock to Thrive

Unlock to Thrive is a web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between education and career readiness for Rwandan students.

Our mission is to empower students to make informed decisions about their future by providing access to personalized skills development resources, contextualized career guidance, simplified mentorship connections, and centralized financial aid information.
